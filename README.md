# 💳 Customer Spending & Revenue Segmentation Analysis

## 📌 Problem Statement

Understanding customer spending behavior is crucial for financial institutions to optimize revenue, improve customer targeting, and increase adoption of premium services.

This project analyzes credit card transaction data to identify high-value customer segments, key revenue drivers, and opportunities for business growth.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze revenue distribution across different customer segments
* Identify high-value customers based on income and occupation
* Understand spending behavior across categories
* Provide actionable recommendations to improve revenue and customer engagement

## 📊 Key Insights

* **Blue cards contribute ~83% of total revenue** → Heavy reliance on entry-level customers
* **Businessman & White-collar segments generate highest revenue** → Primary high-value customer groups
* **High-income customers dominate spending** → Strong correlation between income and transaction volume
* **Bills & Entertainment are top spending categories** → Major revenue drivers
* **Swipe transactions account for majority usage (~60–65%)** → Low adoption of digital methods
* **Premium cards (Gold/Platinum) have low contribution** → Untapped upselling opportunity

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Target **high-income and business-class customers** with personalized offers
* Promote **Gold and Platinum cards** to increase revenue per customer
* Encourage **digital transactions (chip/online)** for better engagement and tracking
* Focus marketing efforts on **high-spending categories (Bills & Entertainment)**
* Develop strategies to **upgrade Blue card users to premium tiers**
